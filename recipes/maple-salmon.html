Maple Salmon Recipe

Description

This quick and easy maple salmon recipe is a delicious option for a weeknight dinner. In just under an hour you will have a sweet meal that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound salmon

Steps

  1. Stir together maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, garlic salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  2. Cut salmon into 4 equal fillets, place in a shallow glass baking dish, and coat with maple syrup mixture. Cover the dish and marinate salmon in refrigerator for 30 minutes, turning once halfway.
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  4. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake salmon uncovered until flesh easily flakes with a fork, about 20 minutes.
